About FreeSip

FreeSip, powered by Arctic Aromas Corporation, is an innovative offline marketing and brand-activation platform designed to bring your brand directly into real-world environments.

What We Do

  • Premium Custom-Branded Water Bottles
    We print your product, service, or promotional message on our high-quality bottles and distribute them directly to your exact target audience. Your brand enters events, companies, workplaces, homes, gyms, offices, and daily routines—creating continuous, natural visibility.
  • Workforce-Based Advertising (1,000+ Volunteers)
    We operate a large network of 1,000+ volunteers across assembly, logistics & manufacturing sectors. We print your brand on premium T-shirts and provide them to these workers, turning their daily routine into a moving billboard for your brand.
  • Powerful Offline Brand Servicing
    In a world full of digital ads that people skip, ignore, or forget—FreeSip delivers offline exposure that people physically experience, trust, and remember.

With FreeSip, your brand moves beyond screens and becomes a part of people’s everyday life.
